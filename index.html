Things to do

Visit Beautiful waterfall

2.5 km from our Bed & Breakfast house you can find magical waterfalls and during the summer days you can take swim in nature environment. You can only take a walk to thewaterfalls because there is no roud that goes nearby, but the walking roud through the wood is absolutly thing you should do while you are here.

Enjoy horse riding

Hippodrome is hour walk-distance awey from B&B House and 20 min by car. You can book 1 hour horse ride thro the woods and feelds neer river.

Pet lovely Alpacas

Alpaca is a species of South American camelid. It is similar to, and often confused with, the llama. However, alpacas are often noticeably smaller than llamas. They are adorable and love to be feed and pet. They are just to cute. The Alpaca farm is 1 km awey from B&B House

Climb to the viewpoint

For those who love intensive walking you can go to the top of the hill where you have beutifull view of your surounding. On top is a small reataurant where you can rest. The walk in one direction is about 6km.
